Agriculture during the early stages of Sangam period was primitive. it progressively got more efficient with improvements in irrigation, ploughing, manuring, storage and distribution.

TYPES OF FARMERS

  1. Marginal Farmers
    2. Farmers who have less than 1 hectare of land are called Marginal farmer
  2. Small Farmers
    3. Farmers who have 1 or 2 hectare of land are called small farmers.
  3. Semi-medium farmer
    4. Farmers who have 2 to 4 hectare of land are called semi medium farmers.
